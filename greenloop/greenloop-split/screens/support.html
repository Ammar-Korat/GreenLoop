Frequently Asked Questions
How is my payout calculated? ▼
Your payout is estimated based on material type and entered quantities. The final amount is confirmed after our team weighs your materials at pickup. Prices update regularly based on market rates.
How long does pickup take? ▼
Pickup is typically scheduled within 1–3 business days after you submit your request. You'll receive an SMS confirmation with your exact pickup window.
Which cities does GreenLoop serve? ▼
We currently serve Cairo, Giza, and Alexandria with more cities launching soon. If your city isn't listed, you can use the "Drop Off" option at a partner center.
Is there a minimum weight requirement? ▼
Yes — a minimum of 5 kg of mixed materials per pickup request. For self-delivery drop-offs, there's no minimum weight.
How secure is my data? ▼
Your personal data and payment info are encrypted using AES-256. We never sell or share your data with third parties.
Can businesses use GreenLoop? ▼
Absolutely! We offer business accounts with scheduled recurring pickups, bulk pricing, and monthly impact reports. Contact us at business@greenloop.eg for custom plans.
